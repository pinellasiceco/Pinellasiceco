Methodology

Data source: Florida Department of Business and Professional Regulation (DBPR) 3FDInspi inspection extract, Pinellas County. Years analyzed: 2018, 2019, 2025, 2026. Analysis includes routine food service inspections only. V22 violations (food contact surfaces: cleaned and sanitized) used as the primary indicator. Ice machine citations identified through inspector observation text analysis of individual DBPR inspection reports. Sample size: 13,691 Pinellas County routine food service inspections. All data sourced from Florida public records. ATP comparison data sourced from published third-party research using Hygiena ATP monitoring systems. Pinellas Ice Co is not affiliated with the Florida DBPR.
